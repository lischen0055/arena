HORST Arts & Music

HORST Arts & Music is a two-day celebration of art and music in a setting like no other. The festival collaborates with an array of talented artists and offers a well-selected worldwide collage of electronic music and in situ visual arts. Nestled away in the green Hageland hills, Belgium you’ll find the 15th century moated castle of Horst. An inspiring space for site-specific art and the perfect festive backdrop.

Horst: The Festival That Blends Music, Art, and Architecture

From 2014 to 2018, Horst Arts & Music took place on the idyllic Castle grounds in Holsbeek. It grew into an internationally renowned platform for showcasing cutting-edge electronic music, inventive in-situ art, and architecture. Horst aligned a three-day immersive music festival with longer-term trajectories in which architects were invited to create unique stages and dancefloors, and in which contemporary artists were commissioned to enter into dialogue with their surroundings through site-specific new productions. - arch daily

Dîner en blanc - mass picnic

Dîner en blanc or Fête en blanc is a community picnic, which takes place in many different communities around the world. It's common in big cities as well as in small villages (also in the rural areas around Munich). All the people come together -dressed completely in white- , bring their food and have a shared dinner. This tradition originated in Paris and is a kind of social dining and "mass picnic".

Fête des Lumières, community dinner

Festival of Lights, is an annual outdoor community dinner in my hometown. It's organised by the "Freundeskreis Samoreau & Bernried", a club which celebrates and cultivates the friendship between my hometown in Germany and our partner community in France. All the people from our village come together and spend a nice evening in an inviting atmosphere, have dinner together and get in touch with each other.

KULTURSPEKTAKEL GAUTING

"Kulturspektakel is an annual small, regional open-air-festival, which takes place sine 1982. During the festival-weekend, more than 40 bands from the region perform. Additionally there is a broad offer of workshops, exhibitions and food. Entrance is free!
Das Kulturspektakel Gauting e.V. is a non-profit association supporting the local art and culture scene. Our festival has no commercial intentions, but only works towards maintaining and improving the event. Admission to the cultural spectacle is free. The costs of the event are generated solely through the sale of drinks and food.
Adolescents and young adults from Gauting and the surrounding area organize the event on a purely voluntary basis. We want to offer a platform to realize ideas and learn new things!

The community of Gauting, many associations and private individuals support us in our project. "
